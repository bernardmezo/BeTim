Discover and support local Indonesian businesses with BeTim, a sleek and modern UMKM (Usaha Mikro, Kecil, dan Menengah) directory built with Next.js, React, and TypeScript. This web application makes it easy to find unique products and services offered by small businesses in your community, fostering economic growth and connection. BeTim provides a user-friendly platform to explore local entrepreneurs and contribute to their success as part of the Web In Action 2025 initiative.
